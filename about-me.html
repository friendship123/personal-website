Sophie's Story

Hi my name is Sophie Raglan and I'm a middle schooler who lives in New York city. I'm a girl with dreams as big as the world.

This Is My Story

I have always been a city girl, I've never known anything else— the streets, traffic, noises and loud lights. The city is always moving and busy just like my life. My favorite phrase is "the city never sleeps" because it is true and is honestly like me. I'm always moving and busy, living life to the fullest. Now I'm a middle schooler with too much homework. I play alot of sports so im busy after school. There are never enough hours in the day for me. I played tennis all my life, but now I have a sudden passion for volleyball, a sport I didn't even think i liked! I have so many great friends on the tema that are all so supportive. This is me, sassy Sophie Raglan.

hobbies/intrestes

Life is so great when you can code; hacking and making websites is so cool when you see what you can accomplish. Sitting at a desk with a messy bun, sweatpants and my laptop makes me always feel at home. My whole life I have also been intrested in making companies, so i thought "how could I make this passion come to life" and thats when I started thinking of real companies not just garage slaes and lemonade stands. I came up with a company called Pocketchange wich is a job site tailored toward teens searching for jobs.

Sophie's Values

We never have enought time in this life. We get one chance to live one life to the fullest and no one knows what happens next. So guess I value life and adventure. I could go on and on about my food on the table and a roof over my head but I'm going to be really specific: I value my grandma—she is so important to me. She helps me throught thick and thin. I value time—time because it controls us all and its fascinating how time gets away from us. Finally, I value opprotunties. There is so little time to waste so I figure you can't have any missed opportunitites.
